Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

1. Introduction

TasteLocal Pte. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data — name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Booking data — experience details, dates, guest counts, and payment confirmation records.
  • Review data — ratings and written reviews you submit.
  • Geolocation data — your device's latitude and longitude, collected only when you use the "Explore Nearby Experiences" feature and only with your explicit browser permission. Location data is used solely to display nearby listings and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — page views, search queries, and click events collected via server logs to improve the platform.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and confirm bookings.
  • To communicate booking updates, receipts, and service announcements.
  • To display personalised experience recommendations.
  • To improve platform features based on aggregated usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as necessary to deliver our services:

  • Hosts receive your name and booking details to prepare for your experience.
  • Payment processors receive payment card data to process transactions. TasteLocal does not store full card numbers.
  • Service providers (e.g. email delivery, cloud hosting) operate under data processing agreements.

5. Geolocation

The "Explore Nearby Experiences" button requests your browser's geolocation permission. This is entirely optional. If you decline, you can still browse all experiences manually. Coordinates passed to the nearby search are used only to compute distances in the current request and are not logged or stored.

6. Cookies

TasteLocal uses session cookies to keep you logged in and CSRF tokens to protect form submissions. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Booking records are kept for 7 years for financial compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your account at any time; we will anonymise or delete personal data within 30 days subject to legal retention obligations.

8. Your Rights

Under the PDPA you have the right to access, correct, and withdraw consent for the use of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at our contact page.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified by email to registered users. Continued use of TasteLocal after notification constitutes acceptance.

Questions about your data? Contact us.
