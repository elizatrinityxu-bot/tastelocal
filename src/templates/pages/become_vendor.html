Who Can Apply?

We welcome applications from anyone with a genuine passion for Singapore food culture — you don't need a commercial kitchen or a chef's qualification. You do need:

  • A food experience that is genuinely unique or local
  • The ability to host guests safely and reliably
  • Good communication skills (English proficiency helps)
  • A Singapore-based operation
