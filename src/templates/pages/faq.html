Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Booking & Payments

Browse the listings, click on any experience you like, and hit Book Now. Select your preferred date and number of guests, then confirm. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately. A TasteLocal account is required to book.

TasteLocal accepts major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as PayNow for Singapore-based users. Payment is processed securely at the time of booking.

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the experience start time are fully refunded. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee at the host's discretion. To reschedule, contact your host directly through the booking details page.

All prices displayed on TasteLocal are per person. The total cost is calculated at checkout based on the number of guests you select.

Experiences & Hosts

Every host completes an application and an identity verification process before their listing goes live. Our team personally reviews each experience to ensure it meets our standards for authenticity, safety, and quality.

Most experiences are conducted in English. Some hosts also offer tours in Mandarin, Malay, or Tamil — this is noted in the listing description. If you have a language preference, contact the host before booking.

Many hosts can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, halal, or gluten-free requirements with advance notice. Check the listing description or message the host directly before booking to confirm.

If a host cancels your booking, you will receive a full refund automatically. We will also notify you by email and, where possible, suggest comparable alternative experiences on the same date.
