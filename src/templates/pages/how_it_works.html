How It Works

Everything you need to know — whether you're here to eat or to host.

For Food Explorers

1

Search & Discover

Browse experiences by cuisine, neighbourhood, price, or rating. Use the map view to find something happening near you right now.

2

Book Instantly

Choose your date, select the number of guests, and confirm your booking in seconds. You'll receive an email confirmation straight away.

3

Show Up & Eat

Meet your host at the agreed location and enjoy an authentic, guided food experience. No preparation required — just bring your appetite.

4

Leave a Review

Help the community by sharing your experience. Your review helps future travellers and rewards great hosts.

For Experience Hosts

1

Apply to Host

Submit a short application telling us about your food background and the experience you want to offer. Our team reviews every application personally.

2

Create Your Listing

Once approved, build your experience page — title, description, photos, pricing, and availability. Our onboarding team is on hand to help.

3

Welcome Guests

Receive booking notifications, manage your schedule from your dashboard, and host food lovers from around the world.

4

Get Paid

Earnings are processed after each completed booking. Transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
