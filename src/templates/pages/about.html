About TasteLocal

Our mission, our story, our people.

Our Mission

TasteLocal exists to bridge the gap between curious travellers and the passionate local hosts who know Singapore's food culture inside out. We believe that the best way to understand a city is through its food — not from a tourist menu, but from a hawker stall run by the same family for three generations, or a home kitchen where a grandmother still grinds her own spice paste by hand.

Every experience on TasteLocal is designed, hosted, and led by vetted local guides and vendors. We handle the booking logistics so hosts can focus on what they do best: sharing extraordinary food and the stories behind it.

Our Story

TasteLocal was founded in Singapore in 2024 by a team of food lovers who were frustrated that visitors kept missing the best bites in the city. Armed with a shared Google Doc of hidden hawker gems and a conviction that food tourism could be done better, we built the first version of the platform in a weekend.

Today we partner with over 50 hosts across Singapore — from Michelin-starred hawker stall owners to third-generation curry puff bakers — offering more than 200 curated experiences.
